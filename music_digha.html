BTS

BTS(Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a seven member internationally renowned korean boyband. It consists of RM (The leader), Jin, Suga, JHope, Jimin, V and Jungkook. The band debuted in 2013 and since then has issued sold out millions of copies of their albums like "BE", "Map of the soul:7", "Love Yourself" etc. Their songs basically work on the theme of making the listener accept and love themselves the way they are. To me BTS is my life saviour. It gave me hope in my darkest times.
